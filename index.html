about me_

Hello there! I'm Izzy :) Glad you're here!

I’ve been designing websites and apps for 4+ years, across strategy, UX, visual design, and handoff. Good work, but I kept getting curious about what happened after the file left my hands.

So I started building the things myself. With Framer, Webflow, code, and AI tools — and now, with a mix of all of them I’m still figuring out.

Turns out the line between “designer who builds” and “builder who designs” is much blurrier than anyone told me.

What I’m doing now feels less like a job description and more like an ongoing experiment: how much of a product can one person own, from architecture to live URL, when the tools keep getting better at closing the gap?

I don’t have a clean answer yet. But I’ve shipped enough to know the question is worth asking.
