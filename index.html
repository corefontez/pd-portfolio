about me_

Hey there! I’m Izzy Fontez. I’m glad you’re here! 🤗

I’ve been designing websites and apps for about 4 years, strategy, UX, visual design. Good work. But I kept getting curious about what happened after the file left my hands, so I started building the things myself: Framer, Webflow, code, AI tools, and a mix of all of them I’m still figuring out.

Turns out the line between “designer who builds” and “builder who designs” is much blurrier than anyone told me.

If you’re building something and want someone who thinks in systems and likes getting their hands in the actual thing, I’d love to talk.
